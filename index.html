About Us

Hi! Im Gianella Im from Argentina, and for the past two years Ive been offering private lessons to children between the ages of 6 and 10 who live in the United States. I help them with their daily homework, we practice reading in English, and reinforce what they learn at school. In 2023, I had an amazing experience living in the U.S., where I immersed myself in the culture and improved my English a lot. When I returned, I realized how much I enjoy teaching and connecting with kids. Ive loved learning since I was little, and now that passion has turned into a way to support others on their own learning journey. I take each class seriously, while also creating a fun, warm, and friendly space where kids feel comfortable and excited to learn.

Id love to be part of your childs learning journey!
