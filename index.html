About Us

Hi! I'm Giane, a 21-year-old tutor from Argentina currently studying International Trade at university. Over the past year, I’ve discovered a true passion for working with kids. I tutor students (ages 6–10) in the U.S., helping them strengthen their reading and math skills in a fun, supportive environment. What makes my lessons special? I combine patience, encouragement, and clear explanations to make learning less stressful and more enjoyable. Whether we’re practicing reading out loud or working through homework step by step, I’m here to help your child feel confident and proud of their progress.

I’d love to be part of your child’s learning journey!
