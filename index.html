About Us

Hi! I’m Giane, a 21-year-old tutor from Argentina. I’m currently studying International Trade at university — a field I truly enjoy.

Over the past year, I’ve discovered how much I love working with children. I’ve been tutoring students (ages 6–10) in the U.S., helping them improve their reading and math skills in a fun, engaging way.

My goal is to support your child’s learning journey with care, patience, and personalized attention.

Looking forward to meeting your little learner!

Services

Build reading confidence

Through engaging activities and personalized support, I help children become confident readers who enjoy the process of learning. We focus on fluency, comprehension, and vocabulary in a stress-free environment.

Strengthen math skills

Using games, step-by-step guidance, and positive reinforcement, I help students build a strong foundation in math. We work on problem-solving, number sense, and critical thinking in a fun, encouraging way.
